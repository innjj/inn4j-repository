[inn4j maven repository](http://inn4j-repository.googlecode.com/svn/repo/)

### Adding to your Maven2/3 application: ###
```
    <repositories>
        ...
        <repository>
            <id>inn4j-repo</id>
            <name>inn4j-repository</name>
            <url>http://inn4j-repository.googlecode.com/svn/repo/</url>
        </repository>
    </repositories>
```
